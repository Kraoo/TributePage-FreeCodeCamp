Magnus Carlsen - World Chess Champion

Biography

Magnus Carlsen, born on November 30, 1990, in Tonsberg, Norway, is a Norwegian chess grandmaster who has been World Chess Champion since 2013. Carlsen was a chess prodigy, becoming a grandmaster at the age of 13 years and 148 days, one of the youngest in history.

Timeline of Magnus Carlsen's Life

  • 1990: Born in Tonsberg, Norway
  • 2004: Became a Grandmaster at the age of 13
  • 2009: Became the youngest player to be ranked world No. 1 at the age of 18
  • 2010: Became the youngest player to win the Chess Oscars
  • 2013: Became the World Chess Champion
  • 2014: Defended his World Chess Championship title
  • 2016: Won the Grand Chess Tour
  • 2018: Became the World Rapid Chess Champion
  • 2019: Became the World Blitz Chess Champion
  • 2020: Successfully defended his World Chess Championship title again
  • 2021: Achieved highest peak Elo rating in history (2882)
